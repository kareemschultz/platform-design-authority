# Agriculture Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Farms, growers, cooperatives, livestock operators, agribusinesses, packhouses, input suppliers, and agricultural service organizations.

## Required Capabilities

- Locations, fields, plots, farms, barns, storage sites, and production units
- Product Catalog crops, livestock, inputs, feed, chemicals, packaging, and outputs
- Inventory lots, batches, expiry, storage conditions, transfers, and traceability
- Procurement seed, feed, chemicals, equipment, supplier contracts, and seasonal purchasing
- Projects and scheduling planting, treatment, harvesting, breeding, and seasonal work
- Assets, fleet, machinery, maintenance, fuel, meters, and inspections
- Workforce crews, seasonal labor, skills, attendance, leave, and payroll inputs
- Manufacturing or processing hooks for grading, packing, blending, milling, or transformation
- Commerce and logistics sales contracts, deliveries, weights, quality grades, and proof of delivery
- Finance cost centers, crop or herd costing, budgets, grants, insurance, and profitability
- Governance certifications, chemical records, welfare, food safety, and audit evidence

## Default Workspaces

Farm Manager, Agronomist, Herd Manager, Field Supervisor, Equipment Operator, Storekeeper, Buyer, Quality Officer, Finance Manager, and Executive.

## Default Workflows

Season planning, field or herd setup, input issue, treatment record, work assignment, harvest or production receipt, grading, packout, storage, dispatch, equipment maintenance, incident reporting, and traceability recall.

## Default Dashboards

Yield, input cost, labor usage, equipment downtime, stock and expiry, field or herd performance, harvest progress, quality grade, order fulfillment, and gross margin by production unit.

## AI Skills

Season-plan assistance, input-demand forecasting, anomaly detection, maintenance prediction, yield and cost forecasting, work prioritization, compliance-record review, and management briefings.
