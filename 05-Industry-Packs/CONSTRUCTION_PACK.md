# Construction Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

General contractors, subcontractors, specialty trades, builders, civil contractors, maintenance contractors, and project-based construction firms.

## Required Capabilities

- CRM leads, bids, opportunities, customer and subcontractor relationships
- Projects estimates, budgets, work breakdown, tasks, milestones, risks, changes, and progress
- Procurement requisitions, quotes, purchase orders, subcontract commitments, and supplier performance
- Inventory tools, materials, site stock, transfers, issues, returns, and counts
- Assets, fleet, equipment assignment, inspections, maintenance, fuel, and utilization
- Workforce workers, crews, skills, certifications, attendance, leave, and payroll inputs
- Service and field work orders, dispatch, appointments, checklists, and proof of completion
- Finance job costing, commitments, progress billing, retention, payables, expenses, and profitability
- Documents contracts, drawings, specifications, permits, RFIs, submittals, and controlled revisions
- Governance safety, incidents, corrective actions, compliance evidence, and audits

## Default Workspaces

Estimator, Project Manager, Site Supervisor, Foreman, Field Worker, Buyer, Equipment Manager, Safety Officer, Quantity Surveyor, Project Accountant, and Executive.

## Default Workflows

Lead-to-bid, estimate approval, project setup, subcontract award, material request, site transfer, daily log, inspection, RFI, submittal, change order, progress claim, timesheet approval, safety incident, and project closeout.

## Default Dashboards

Project margin, budget versus actual, commitments, change exposure, schedule health, labor productivity, equipment utilization, safety incidents, overdue RFIs and submittals, billing status, and cash forecast.

## AI Skills

Estimate assistance, contract and specification summarization, risk identification, daily-log drafting, schedule-delay explanation, cost-to-complete forecasting, safety trend analysis, and document comparison.
