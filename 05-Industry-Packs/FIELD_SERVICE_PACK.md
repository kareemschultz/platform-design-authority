# Field Service Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Repair, installation, inspection, utilities, maintenance, home services, telecom, security, and mobile technical-service businesses.

## Required Capabilities

- Service cases, work orders, dispatch, appointments, SLAs, contracts, and warranties
- Scheduling, skills, territories, routes, availability, and travel time
- Workforce technicians, certifications, time, attendance, expenses, and payroll inputs
- Inventory vans, depots, parts, reservations, transfers, returns, and serial tracking
- Assets installed base, service history, meters, maintenance plans, and inspections
- Commerce quotes, approvals, customer signatures, deposits, payments, and invoices
- CRM customer, site, contact, communication, and service history
- Fleet vehicles, inspections, fuel, telematics, and compliance where applicable

## Default Workspaces

Dispatcher, Technician, Field Supervisor, Service Manager, Parts Coordinator, Customer Service Representative, Finance Manager, and Executive.

## Default Workflows

Request-to-work-order, triage, schedule, dispatch, travel, arrival, diagnosis, quote approval, parts issue, completion, customer sign-off, invoicing, warranty claim, and follow-up.

## Default Dashboards

First-time fix rate, SLA performance, travel time, utilization, backlog, repeat visits, parts availability, technician productivity, customer satisfaction, and service profitability.

## AI Skills

Triage, scheduling suggestions, technician briefing, knowledge retrieval, probable-cause analysis, parts recommendations, completion-note drafting, and service-risk forecasting.
