# Government and Public Administration Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Municipalities, agencies, ministries, public authorities, councils, utilities, and public-service organizations.

## Required Capabilities

- Organizations, legal entities, departments, delegated authority, public roles, and strong audit
- CRM residents, businesses, applicants, suppliers, stakeholders, and service histories
- Service requests, cases, permits, inspections, complaints, incidents, and public portals
- Procurement requisitions, tenders, evaluations, contracts, purchase orders, and supplier governance
- Projects programs, capital works, grants, milestones, budgets, risks, and outcomes
- Workforce positions, civil-service structures, recruitment, attendance, leave, and payroll inputs
- Finance appropriations, budgets, commitments, funds, payments, assets, and statutory reporting
- Assets, fleet, facilities, maintenance, inspections, and public infrastructure records
- Documents records, policies, correspondence, publication, retention, legal hold, and information requests
- Governance obligations, controls, audits, ethics, conflicts, investigations, and evidence

## Default Workspaces

Citizen Service Officer, Case Officer, Inspector, Procurement Officer, Project Manager, Finance Officer, HR Administrator, Records Officer, Compliance Officer, Department Head, and Executive.

## Default Workflows

Public request intake, eligibility review, permit or license approval, inspection, complaint escalation, tender and award, budget approval, payment authorization, records request, policy publication, audit finding, and corrective action.

## Public-Sector Requirements

- Accessibility and multilingual public services
- Transparent decision history and records retention
- Segregation of duties and delegated authority
- Open-data and public-information controls
- Data residency, sovereignty, privacy, and procurement compliance
- Strong offline and field capability for inspections and remote sites

## AI Skills

Case summarization, correspondence drafting, policy retrieval, document classification, service-demand forecasting, procurement review assistance, audit-evidence organization, and executive briefings with mandatory human review.
