# Hospitality Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Hotels, guest houses, resorts, serviced apartments, hostels, conference venues, and mixed lodging and food-service operations.

## Required Capabilities

- Reservations, availability, rates, packages, deposits, cancellations, and guest profiles
- Front desk check-in, room assignment, stay management, folios, and check-out
- Housekeeping rooms, tasks, inspections, linen, minibar, and maintenance requests
- Commerce POS for restaurants, bars, shops, spa, and room charges
- CRM guest history, preferences, consent, loyalty, and communications
- Assets and Maintenance rooms, equipment, preventive work, and downtime
- Workforce rosters, attendance, leave, service roles, and payroll inputs
- Finance receivables, payables, cash, expenses, taxes, and property profitability
- Events, banqueting, meeting spaces, catering, and project or scheduling hooks

## Default Workspaces

Reservations Agent, Front Desk, Concierge, Housekeeping, Maintenance, Food and Beverage, Events Coordinator, Revenue Manager, Finance Manager, General Manager, and Administrator.

## Default Workflows

Reservation-to-stay, deposit and cancellation, check-in, room move, housekeeping turnover, maintenance block, room charge, night audit, check-out, lost property, and guest recovery.

## Default Dashboards

Occupancy, average daily rate, revenue per available room, booking pace, cancellations, room status, housekeeping productivity, guest satisfaction, outlet revenue, maintenance downtime, and property profitability.

## AI Skills

Guest-message drafting, review summarization, occupancy and demand forecasting, rate recommendations, housekeeping prioritization, maintenance-risk alerts, and manager briefings.
