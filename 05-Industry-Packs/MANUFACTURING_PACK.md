# Manufacturing Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Discrete, process, batch, make-to-stock, make-to-order, engineer-to-order, and mixed-mode manufacturers.

## Required Capabilities

- Product Catalog, bills of material, formulas, routings, variants, units, and packaging
- Manufacturing production orders, operations, material consumption, output, quality, scrap, and rework
- Inventory lots, serials, expiry, reservations, transfers, valuation inputs, and traceability
- Warehouse receiving, putaway, picking, staging, kitting, and mobile execution
- Procurement requisitions, sourcing, purchase orders, subcontracting, and supplier quality
- Assets and Maintenance work centers, preventive maintenance, downtime, and calibration
- Workforce skills, shifts, time, certifications, and payroll inputs
- Finance costing, variance, fixed assets, budgets, and profitability
- Planning demand, material requirements, capacity, scenarios, and forecasts

## Default Workspaces

Planner, Production Supervisor, Operator, Quality Inspector, Maintenance Technician, Warehouse Operator, Buyer, Cost Accountant, Plant Manager, and Executive.

## Default Workflows

Demand-to-plan, plan-to-production-order, material issue, operation completion, quality inspection, nonconformance, rework, finished-goods receipt, subcontract processing, maintenance escalation, and production close.

## Default Dashboards

Schedule attainment, throughput, yield, scrap, downtime, work-in-process, material shortages, quality failures, labor efficiency, cost variance, on-time completion, and overall equipment effectiveness inputs.

## AI Skills

Production scheduling recommendations, shortage analysis, anomaly detection, predictive maintenance, quality-root-cause assistance, yield forecasting, and cost-variance explanation.
