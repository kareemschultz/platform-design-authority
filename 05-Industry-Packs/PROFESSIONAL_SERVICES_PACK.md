# Professional Services Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Consultancies, agencies, legal and accounting firms, engineering practices, IT service providers, architects, designers, and other project- or retainer-based service businesses.

## Required Capabilities

- CRM leads, opportunities, accounts, contacts, proposals, and relationship history
- Projects scopes, tasks, milestones, resources, time, expenses, risks, changes, and deliverables
- Workforce employees, contractors, skills, availability, leave, performance, and payroll inputs
- Scheduling meetings, appointments, resource allocation, and recurring service commitments
- Commerce quotes, retainers, deposits, subscriptions, usage, and customer approvals
- Finance time-and-materials, fixed-fee, milestone, retainer, write-off, invoicing, collections, and profitability
- Documents proposals, contracts, statements of work, deliverables, policies, knowledge, and e-signature hooks
- Service cases, managed-service requests, SLAs, support queues, and customer portals
- Marketing thought leadership, campaigns, events, nurturing, and referral tracking
- Planning pipeline, utilization, capacity, revenue forecast, and scenario analysis

## Default Workspaces

Business Development, Consultant or Practitioner, Project Manager, Resource Manager, Engagement Partner, Service Desk, Finance Manager, Knowledge Manager, Executive, and Client Portal.

## Default Workflows

Lead-to-proposal, conflict or eligibility review, proposal approval, contract and e-signature, project setup, resource request, time and expense approval, change request, milestone acceptance, invoice review, collection, and engagement close.

## Default Dashboards

Pipeline, backlog, utilization, realization, billable time, project margin, resource capacity, work in progress, unbilled revenue, receivables aging, client profitability, and forecast accuracy.

## AI Skills

Proposal drafting, scope and contract summarization, resource matching, timesheet assistance, project-risk detection, meeting summaries, knowledge retrieval, invoice narrative generation, and profitability forecasting.
