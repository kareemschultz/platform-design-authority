# Salon and Personal Services Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Salons, spas, barbershops, nail studios, wellness centers, beauty clinics, tattoo studios, and appointment-based personal-service businesses.

## Required Capabilities

- CRM clients, preferences, consent, service history, memberships, and communication
- Scheduling appointments, resources, rooms, waitlists, reminders, deposits, and cancellation rules
- Product Catalog services, durations, add-ons, packages, retail products, and staff skill requirements
- Commerce POS, tips, split payments, gift cards, store credit, packages, memberships, refunds, and receipts
- Inventory retail stock, consumables, transfers, counts, expiry, and replenishment
- Workforce staff, skills, rosters, attendance, leave, commission inputs, and payroll inputs
- Marketing campaigns, referrals, loyalty, promotions, rebooking, and win-back journeys
- Finance cash, sales, expenses, commissions, taxes, payables, and location profitability
- Documents consent forms, treatment notes, policies, waivers, and acknowledgements where applicable

## Default Workspaces

Receptionist, Service Professional, Shift Manager, Inventory Coordinator, Marketing Manager, Finance Manager, Owner, and Customer Booking Portal.

## Default Workflows

Online booking, deposit, check-in, consultation or consent, service delivery, retail upsell, checkout, tip, rebooking, cancellation, no-show, package redemption, membership renewal, and stock replenishment.

## Default Dashboards

Bookings, utilization, rebooking rate, no-shows, average ticket, retail attachment, staff productivity, commissions, membership retention, inventory usage, and location profitability.

## AI Skills

Booking assistance, personalized service and product suggestions, rebooking outreach, schedule-gap filling, review response drafting, demand forecasting, inventory recommendations, and owner briefings.
