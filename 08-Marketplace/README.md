# Marketplace

Planned specifications:

- Marketplace architecture and governance
- Publisher onboarding and verification
- Extension manifests and permissions
- Installation, upgrade, suspension, and removal
- Billing, revenue share, refunds, and settlement
- Security review and sandboxing
- Data access and privacy disclosures
- Compatibility and certification
- Ratings, reviews, support, and disputes
- Themes, integrations, applications, AI tools, and industry content
- Customer and partner curation

Marketplace content must use published contracts and may not create hidden database access or bypass platform policy.
