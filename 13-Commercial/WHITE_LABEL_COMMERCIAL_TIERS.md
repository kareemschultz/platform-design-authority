# White Label Commercial Tiers

## Purpose

Define how branding, domains, application identity, support presentation, communications, documents, partner controls, and mobile packaging are sold without creating fragmented product forks.

## Tiers

### Brand Essentials

Logo, color tokens, favicon, document headers, email footer, and organization identity.

### Branded Experience

Custom login, themes, terminology, documents, notifications, support details, and selected portal presentation.

### Full White Label

Verified custom domain, sender identities, approved application naming, branded knowledge and support surfaces, advanced theme controls, and removal of ordinary platform branding where contractually permitted.

### Platform Partner

Partner-controlled customer hierarchy, partner brand inheritance, customer catalog, delegated administration, partner support portal, revenue share, implementation controls, and optional branded mobile distribution.

## Rules

1. White-label rights are entitlement-controlled and versioned.
2. Branding changes never alter security semantics, state meaning, legal notices, accessibility, or tenant isolation.
3. Custom domains require verification, certificate lifecycle, cookie policy, OAuth callback, passkey relying-party, and anti-phishing review.
4. Mobile white-labeling uses one governed codebase and build pipeline.
5. Customer or partner assets remain isolated and malware-scanned.
6. Downgrade defines domain, app, email, document, and asset fallback behavior.
7. Platform attribution requirements must be explicit by tier and jurisdiction.

## Commercial Dimensions

- One-time implementation fee
- Recurring white-label platform fee
- Number of brands, domains, or mobile app variants
- Build, store-submission, certificate, and maintenance fees
- Premium support and release-coordination requirements

## Exit Behavior

Contracts must define domain transfer, app-store ownership, certificate handover, asset export, customer communication, and continued access when a partner relationship ends.
