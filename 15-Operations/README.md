# Operations

Planned specifications:

- Service ownership and operating model
- Observability and service-level objectives
- Incident, problem, and change management
- Runbooks and escalation
- Backup, restore, disaster recovery, and continuity
- Capacity, performance, and cost management
- Tenant operations and support access
- Data repair and reconciliation
- Security operations
- Release and environment operations
- Status communication
- Partner and self-hosted operational responsibilities
- Operational readiness reviews

Every production capability requires an owner, telemetry, failure modes, runbooks, support path, and recovery evidence.
