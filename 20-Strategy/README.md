# Strategy

Planned specifications:

- Company and platform strategy
- Market segmentation and beachhead selection
- Competitive-intelligence program
- Direct competitor and best-in-class benchmark framework
- Platform Experience Index
- Business DNA strategy
- Product portfolio and edition strategy
- Geographic and jurisdiction expansion
- Partner, ecosystem, academy, and certification strategy
- Build, buy, partner, and integrate decisions
- Strategic risks and assumptions
- Long-term platform and company milestones

Strategy guides prioritization but must not bypass architecture, security, evidence, or lifecycle governance.
