# Contribution Policy

External code or documentation contributions are not currently accepted.

This repository is publicly visible under the provisional disclosure posture in FDR-005. It has no source-code or documentation license, and public visibility does not grant permission to copy, modify, redistribute, publish packages, or use the internal Meridian codename commercially. Copyright ownership, contributor terms, source/documentation licensing, and public package scope remain unresolved and require Founder and qualified legal review.

Public issues may be used only for non-sensitive defects or governed proposals through the supplied templates. Do not submit:

- security vulnerabilities, exploit steps, proof-of-concept payloads, or incident evidence;
- secrets, credentials, private URLs, customer or tenant data, personal information, or unredacted logs;
- private provider, legal, commercial, licensing, or premium-source material;
- source or assets whose provenance and redistribution rights are not established.

See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for the security-reporting boundary and [AGENTS.md](AGENTS.md) for the repository's governance contract. External contribution intake may open only after FDR-005, FDR-002, contributor terms, licensing, provenance, and private security-reporting controls are ratified.
