# Automotive Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Repair shops, service centers, dealerships, parts retailers, tire shops, body shops, fleets, inspection centers, and automotive distributors.

## Required Capabilities

- CRM customers, drivers, vehicles, communication, estimates, and service history
- Service work orders, appointments, inspections, technician assignment, warranties, and approvals
- Product Catalog parts, labor operations, tires, fluids, kits, fitment, and supplier identifiers
- Inventory parts, serials, lots, bins, reservations, transfers, cores, and returns
- Procurement purchase orders, supplier catalogs, special orders, and claims
- Assets and Maintenance workshop equipment, lifts, tools, calibration, and safety checks
- Commerce POS, estimates, deposits, payments, invoices, returns, and customer authorization
- Workforce technicians, skills, certifications, time, attendance, and payroll inputs
- Fleet hooks for commercial vehicle customers and internal loaner vehicles

## Default Workspaces

Service Advisor, Technician, Parts Advisor, Workshop Controller, Inspector, Cashier, Buyer, Warranty Administrator, Finance Manager, and General Manager.

## Default Workflows

Appointment, vehicle check-in, inspection, estimate, customer approval, parts reservation, repair execution, quality check, road test, invoicing, payment, warranty claim, and follow-up.

## Default Dashboards

Workshop capacity, technician efficiency, hours sold versus worked, parts fill rate, estimate conversion, comeback rate, average repair order, warranty recovery, inventory aging, and gross profit.

## AI Skills

Inspection-note drafting, probable-cause assistance, estimate explanation, parts suggestions, service reminder generation, technician briefing, warranty-document review, and profitability analysis.
