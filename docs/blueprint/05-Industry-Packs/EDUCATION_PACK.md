# Education Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Schools, training centers, vocational institutions, tutoring businesses, academies, and education-support organizations.

## Required Capabilities

- CRM learners, guardians, sponsors, alumni, and institutional relationships
- Applications, admissions, enrollment, classes, cohorts, schedules, attendance, and progression hooks
- Commerce fees, deposits, invoicing, payments, refunds, scholarships, and account statements
- Workforce faculty, staff, qualifications, leave, attendance, and payroll inputs
- Projects and scheduling for courses, programs, events, and resource allocation
- Assets classrooms, laboratories, devices, equipment, maintenance, and assignments
- Documents policies, consent, forms, certificates, transcripts, records, and acknowledgements
- Marketing recruitment campaigns, inquiries, events, and nurturing
- Governance safeguarding, privacy, incidents, complaints, and compliance evidence
- Reporting enrollment, attendance, completion, financial, staffing, and operational outcomes

## Default Workspaces

Admissions Officer, Registrar, Instructor, Student Services, Finance Officer, HR Administrator, Facilities Manager, Compliance Officer, Executive, Learner Portal, and Guardian Portal.

## Default Workflows

Inquiry-to-enrollment, application review, offer and acceptance, fee setup, class assignment, attendance exception, leave or deferral, certificate issuance, refund, complaint, and alumni transition.

## Boundaries

A full learning-management system, assessment engine, or regulated student-information system may require dedicated domain specifications. This pack establishes the shared operational foundation.

## AI Skills

Application summarization, learner communication drafting, schedule assistance, attendance-risk alerts, policy retrieval, operational reporting, and document generation with human review.
