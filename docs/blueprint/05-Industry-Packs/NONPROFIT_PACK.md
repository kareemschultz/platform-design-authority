# Nonprofit Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Charities, foundations, associations, faith-based organizations, community groups, NGOs, membership organizations, and grant-funded programs.

## Required Capabilities

- CRM donors, members, beneficiaries, volunteers, partners, and institutional funders
- Fundraising campaigns, appeals, pledges, recurring gifts, receipts, and stewardship
- Commerce events, memberships, merchandise, fees, payments, and refunds
- Projects programs, grants, outcomes, milestones, budgets, and delivery tracking
- Workforce employees, volunteers, assignments, skills, leave, and payroll inputs
- Finance funds, restrictions, grants, budgets, expenses, payables, banking, and reporting
- Marketing segments, communications, consent, journeys, and engagement
- Documents policies, grant agreements, donor acknowledgements, evidence, and reports
- Governance boards, risks, controls, incidents, audits, conflicts, and compliance

## Default Workspaces

Fundraising Officer, Program Manager, Volunteer Coordinator, Membership Officer, Finance Manager, Grant Manager, Executive Director, Board or Committee Portal, and Administrator.

## Default Workflows

Donor onboarding, donation and receipt, pledge collection, grant application, grant award, restricted-fund spending, volunteer onboarding, program delivery, outcome reporting, board approval, and annual reporting.

## Default Dashboards

Fundraising progress, donor retention, recurring revenue, restricted versus available funds, grant utilization, program outcomes, volunteer activity, membership renewal, operating runway, and compliance deadlines.

## AI Skills

Grant-opportunity summarization, proposal drafting, donor communication, stewardship suggestions, program-report drafting, outcome analysis, policy retrieval, and board briefing preparation.
