# Restaurant and Food Service Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Quick-service restaurants, full-service restaurants, cafes, bars, bakeries, food trucks, cloud kitchens, catering businesses, and multi-location food-service groups.

## Required Capabilities

- POS, tables, tabs, split bills, modifiers, tips, discounts, refunds, and offline selling
- Menus, recipes, variants, combos, allergens, nutrition, and channel publication
- Inventory ingredients, units, yield, waste, expiry, transfers, and counts
- Procurement suppliers, purchase orders, receiving, and price variance
- Workforce shifts, attendance, leave, skills, and payroll inputs
- Finance sales, cash, expenses, tax, payables, and profitability
- E-commerce ordering, delivery, pickup, reservations, and marketplace integrations

## Default Workspaces

Cashier, Server, Kitchen Expediter, Bartender, Shift Manager, Store Manager, Inventory or Kitchen Manager, Buyer, Finance Manager, and Executive.

## Default Workflows

Order-to-kitchen, table service, takeout, delivery, void approval, comp approval, shift close, cash reconciliation, recipe depletion, waste logging, supplier receiving, and end-of-day review.

## Default Dashboards

Sales by channel, average ticket, labor percentage, food cost, waste, voids and comps, table turn, preparation time, order backlog, stockouts, and location profitability.

## AI Skills

Menu engineering, demand and preparation forecasting, waste analysis, staffing recommendation, anomaly detection, review summarization, and purchasing assistance.
