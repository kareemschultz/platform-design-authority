# Wholesale and Distribution Industry Pack

## Target Organizations

Wholesalers, distributors, importers, exporters, regional suppliers, B2B sellers, and multi-warehouse operators.

## Required Capabilities

- Product Catalog, customer-specific assortments, packaging, supplier codes, and units
- CRM accounts, contacts, opportunities, territories, and account ownership
- Commerce quotes, sales orders, backorders, customer account sales, returns, and deposits
- Pricing contracts, tiers, quantity breaks, and customer-specific rules
- Inventory availability, reservations, transfers, lots, serials, expiry, and replenishment
- Warehouse receiving, putaway, waves, picking, packing, staging, and loading
- Procurement sourcing, purchase orders, tolerances, supplier returns, and scorecards
- Supply Chain shipments, carriers, tracking, freight, proof of delivery, and claims
- Finance receivables, credit, payables, banking, landed cost, and profitability

## Default Workspaces

Sales Representative, Customer Service Representative, Buyer, Warehouse Operator, Warehouse Manager, Dispatcher, Credit Controller, Finance Manager, Executive, and Administrator.

## Default Workflows

- Quote-to-order
- Credit check and order release
- Backorder allocation
- Replenishment and purchase planning
- Container or inbound receipt
- Wave pick, pack, ship, and proof of delivery
- Customer return and supplier claim
- Freight and landed-cost allocation

## Default Dashboards

Sales pipeline, open orders, fill rate, backorders, stock availability, aged inventory, warehouse productivity, on-time delivery, receivables aging, supplier performance, and gross margin by customer or product.

## AI Skills

Demand planning, backorder prioritization, customer credit-risk explanation, purchasing recommendations, route and shipment exception summaries, margin leakage detection, and supplier-performance analysis.
