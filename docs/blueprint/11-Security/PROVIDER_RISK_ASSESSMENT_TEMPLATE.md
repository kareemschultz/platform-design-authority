# Provider Risk Assessment Template

## Purpose

Provide a repeatable assessment for identity, payment, communications, AI, hosting, storage, search, tax, fiscalization, observability, marketplace, and other external providers.

## Provider Profile

- Legal name and contracting entity
- Service and product
- Website and documentation
- Owner and internal sponsor
- Countries, regions, and data locations
- Critical workflows supported
- Alternatives and exit path
- Contract term and renewal

## Data and Privacy

- Data categories and classifications
- Controller, processor, or independent-party role
- Subprocessors
- Data residency and transfers
- Retention and deletion
- Training or secondary use
- Data-subject request support
- Breach notification
- Export and termination

## Security

- Authentication and authorization
- Encryption and key management
- Tenant isolation
- Secure development
- Vulnerability management
- Audit reports and certifications
- Penetration testing
- Incident response
- Support access
- Supply-chain controls

## Reliability and Operations

- Availability commitment
- Latency and capacity
- Regions and failover
- Backup and recovery
- Maintenance and change notice
- Status and incident communication
- Support severity and escalation
- Rate limits and quotas

## Commercial and Legal

- Pricing and usage exposure
- Currency and tax
- Liability and indemnity
- Service credits
- Termination
- Intellectual property
- Regulatory status
- Insurance
- Audit rights

## Capability Matrix

For each needed feature record:

- Supported
- Unsupported
- Undocumented
- Contractual only
- Sandbox verified
- Production certified
- Limitations

Provider marketing statements are not treated as certified capability.

## Lock-In and Exit

- Data and configuration export
- Credential revocation
- Replacement adapter
- Migration duration and cost
- Historical evidence retention
- Customer communication
- Fallback test

## Risk Rating

Rate impact and likelihood for security, privacy, availability, financial correctness, regulatory exposure, lock-in, cost, and operational burden.

## Decision

- Approved
- Approved with conditions
- Prototype only
- Rejected
- Pending evidence

Record conditions, owner, deadline, review date, and residual risk acceptance.
