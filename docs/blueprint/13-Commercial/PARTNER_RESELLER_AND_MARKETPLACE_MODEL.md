# Partner, Reseller, and Marketplace Model

## Purpose

Define how partners sell, implement, brand, support, extend, and earn revenue from the platform while customers retain clear contractual, data, billing, and support relationships.

## Partner Types

- Referral partner
- Authorized reseller
- Managed-service provider
- Implementation partner
- Technology and integration partner
- Marketplace publisher
- Franchise or network operator
- Strategic platform partner

## Commercial Models

- Referral commission
- Wholesale discount and resale margin
- Recurring revenue share
- Implementation and managed-service fees
- Marketplace application fee
- Usage-based publisher share
- Co-sell or negotiated enterprise arrangement

## Rules

1. Every customer must know the contracting, billing, support, and data-controlling parties.
2. Partner authority is delegated, scoped, revocable, and audited.
3. Revenue calculations are based on versioned agreements and settlement records.
4. Refunds, credits, disputes, taxes, chargebacks, and bad debt must define who bears the adjustment.
5. A partner may curate offers but cannot bypass platform minimum security or capability dependencies.
6. Customer portability and partner exit must be designed before activation.
7. Marketplace extensions declare permissions, billing dimensions, support owner, privacy terms, compatibility, and revocation behavior.

## Marketplace Settlement

This settlement model is Phase 3 only and remains disabled until `docs/blueprint/08-Marketplace/MARKETPLACE_COMMERCIAL_PHASING.md`, FDR-008, legal/tax review, and provider gates are complete. If admitted, track gross transaction value, platform fee, publisher share, tax, refunds, chargebacks, reserves, payout status, currency conversion, and accounting references; reconcile with provider transfers and Finance without introducing custody or payment facilitation by implication.

## Partner Lifecycle

Application, due diligence, agreement, enablement, certification, active, monitored, suspended, terminated, and archived.

## Customer Transfer

Support controlled transfer between direct and partner management or between partners, preserving identity, entitlements, billing history, data, custom domains, extensions, and audit evidence.

## Paid-Marketplace Phase Gate

Publisher settlement, tax onboarding, and payout are Phase 3-only design intent gated by `docs/blueprint/08-Marketplace/MARKETPLACE_COMMERCIAL_PHASING.md` and FDR-008. No current phase authorizes custody or merchant-of-record behavior.
