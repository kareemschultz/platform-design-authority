# Competitive Intelligence and Benchmarking

## Purpose

Create a living evidence program that compares the platform against direct ERP and business-platform competitors, best-in-class specialists, and experience leaders.

## Competitor Groups

### Direct Platforms

Odoo, ERPNext, NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics 365, SAP Business One, Acumatica, Zoho, Sage, and relevant regional systems.

### Specialist Products

POS, inventory, warehouse, payroll, CRM, service, payments, accounting, scheduling, and vertical-industry leaders.

### Experience Leaders

Products that set expectations for onboarding, search, collaboration, mobile, developer experience, self-service, and accessibility even when they are not direct competitors.

## Research Record

Every observation records:

- Product and edition
- Date and region
- Source type
- Workflow tested
- User role
- Evidence link or screenshot reference
- Measured result
- Limitation
- Interpretation
- Platform implication

Marketing claims are not treated as verified capability without product, documentation, customer, or implementation evidence.

## Benchmark Dimensions

- Time to first value
- Workflow completion time
- Steps, clicks, typing, and context switches
- Error and correction rate
- Mobile and offline capability
- Accessibility
- Search and navigation
- Configuration and extensibility
- Reporting and analytics
- APIs and ecosystem
- Implementation and migration
- Reliability and performance
- Pricing transparency
- Support burden
- Trust, audit, privacy, and security

## Workflow Benchmarks

Initial benchmarks include:

- Configure a store and import products
- Open register and complete sale
- Process return and refund
- Count and reconcile inventory
- Create supplier and purchase order
- Close cash and prepare deposit
- Add user and role
- Configure custom field
- Build report or dashboard
- Connect an integration
- Operate during internet loss

## Scoring

Use evidence-backed scores with confidence and context. Do not collapse every dimension into one vanity number. Record where the platform chooses not to compete.

## Cadence

- Quarterly market scan
- Annual deep benchmark
- Release-specific competitor review
- Win/loss and implementation feedback
- Customer workflow observations

## Governance

Competitive evidence informs priorities but cannot override architecture, legal requirements, customer research, or product strategy. Copying competitors is not a goal; delivering materially better outcomes is.
